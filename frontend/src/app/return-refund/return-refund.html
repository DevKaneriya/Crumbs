Your satisfaction is our priority. Please read our return and refund policy carefully before placing an order.

Our Return Policy

Since our products are food items, we follow strict hygiene and safety standards. We accept returns only in the following circumstances:

  • The product received is damaged or broken during transit.
  • The wrong item was delivered to you.
  • The product received is past its expiry date.

Returns must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with supporting photographs sent to our customer support team.

Non-Returnable Items

Due to the perishable nature of food products, we are unable to accept returns for:

  • Opened or partially consumed products.
  • Products returned after 48 hours of delivery.
  • Orders where the packaging is intact and product is undamaged.

Refund Process

Once your return request is approved, refunds are processed within 5–7 working days to your original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order ID and photos of the issue.
  2. Our team will review your request and respond within 1–2 working days.
  3. If approved, the refund will be credited to your original payment method.

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placement, provided they have not yet been dispatched. Once an order is shipped, cancellations are not possible. To cancel, contact us immediately via email or phone.
